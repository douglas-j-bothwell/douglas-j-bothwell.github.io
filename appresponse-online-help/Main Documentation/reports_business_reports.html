Business Reports and Business Hours
Business Reports are useful when you want to identify "Business Hours" during the week (such as Mon-Fri 9am-5pm) and report only on those hours. There are two phases to defining a Business Report:
Defining a Business Hours Profile
Defining a Business Report
Defining a Business Hours Profile
To define a Business Hours profile, do the following:
1)
In the Desktop Console, choose Tools > Reporting > Business Hours Manager.
2)
Click New to create a new profile, or select a defined profile in the table.
3)
In the Identity tabbed page, specify the following:
a)
Name = A descriptive label for the profile, such as "27 x 7", "Mon-Fri 9am-5pm", or "Sat-Sun 11am-4pm".
b)
Description
4)
In the Definition tabbed page, do the following:
–
To include a set of hours in the profile, click Select and then click or drag to include the hours of interest.
 
–
To exclude a set of hours in the profile, click De-Select and then click or drag to exclude any hours that are currently included.
 
A Business Report includes hours in a Business Hours profile as follows:
–
Daily reports: includes data for all hours in the corresponding day-of-week
–
Weekly reports: includes data for all hours in entire BH profile
–
Monthly reports: includes data for all hours in all corresponding days-of-week in that month
5)
Click Apply or OK to save the Business Hours profile. You can now use this profile in a Business Report definition, as described in the following procedure.
Defining a Business Report
To define a Business Report, do the following:
1)
In the Desktop Console, choose Tools > Reporting > Report Manager.
2)
In the Identify tabbed page, set the Type to Business Report.
3)
Specify the other options in this tabbed page as you would for a regular report.
4)
Specify the options in the Content and Actions tabbed pages as you would for a regular report.
For more information about report options, see the following section of the AppResponse documentation: Main Documentation > User Guide > Reports > Creating Reports using the Report Manager
5)
In the Schedule page, set the following options:
–
Frequency
–
Business Hours: Select a defined profile, or click the button to open the Business Hours Manager.
–
Time Zone: The time zone to which the Business Hours profile applies.
–
Start Date: The date when the appliance start publishing daily, weekly, or monthly Business Reports
–
End Date (optional)
–
On-Demand Reports: Select the checkbox if you want to enable users to generate on-demand reports using this Business Report definition.
Note—You cannot generate an on-demand report unless the appliance has report data for the entire time window. For example, you cannot generate an on-demand report at 2:00 pm for today because the appliance does not have data for the entire time window (12:00 am - 11:59 pm).
