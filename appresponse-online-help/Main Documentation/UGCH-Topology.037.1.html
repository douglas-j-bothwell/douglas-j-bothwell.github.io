Topology
One of the challenges of network management is making sense of the vast quantities of data carried over the network. The appliance collects many detailed metrics for each group. Group to group data transfers can often be difficult to understand through regular tables and charts. The topology view provides a visualization of group to group relationships, making it much easier to understand how different elements within the network interact.
This view allows you to visualize the topology between three different group types:
1)
Business Groups, including Business Group Containers (see Business Group Topology)
2)
IP Addresses (see IP Topology)
3)
Autonomous Systems (see AS Topology)
