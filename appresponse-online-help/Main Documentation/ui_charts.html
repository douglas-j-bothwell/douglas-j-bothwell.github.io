Charts
Charts are useful for visually analyzing metric information for one or more groups. Many charts can display multiple groups, including groups of different types.
To create a chart in the Desktop Console, do one of the following:
Choose View > Chart > [chart_type]
Clicking on one of the Chart toolbar buttons. A new chart is blank until you drag and drop a group from a table or insight.
To create a new Multi-Group Metric Series Chart from a table, double-click on the cell that shows the group and metric you want to display.
