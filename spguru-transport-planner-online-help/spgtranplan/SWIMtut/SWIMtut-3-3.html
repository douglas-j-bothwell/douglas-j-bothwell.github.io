Conclusion

In this tutorial, you have learned how to define a multi-layer view of an IP-over-WDM network using SP Guru Network Planner and SP Guru Transport Planner. IP links and routes can be inspected across all layers. Their attributes, such as delay and operational status, are automatically updated according to the state of the underlying optical network. Interactive failure analysis across layers helps in assessing the effects of cable breaks and other failure scenarios on the IP layer.
