Evaluating Results

After you dimension the rings and (if needed) design the mesh, you can evaluate the results. You can view results in two different browsers:

  • You can inspect the different rings and how they are utilized in the Ring Browser (see Viewing Ring Details in the Ring Browser).
  • You can inspect the path(s) of a connection—that is, the ring and mesh elements over which the connection is routed—using the Connection Browser (see Viewing Ring Paths in the Connection Browser).
