LOP End-to-End Grooming Algorithm

LOP grooming uses an end-to-end grooming algorithm. This means that each node pair that has LOP traffic requires an equivalent amount of DCL traffic. The exact amount of DCL traffic required depends on the bit rates of the LOP and DCL traffic. The LOP-to-DCL traffic mapping is based on the SONET/SDH multiplexing hierarchy of mapping VT/VC containers into VT groups in STS/STM signals (as described in SONET/SDH Systems).
