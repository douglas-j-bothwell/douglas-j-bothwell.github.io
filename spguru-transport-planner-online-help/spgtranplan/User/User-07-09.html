Pinning and Unpinning Connections

You can pin a connection to fix the route of that connection and ensure that the connection is not affected when you tear down the traffic. This enables you to do a "partial tear-down" of a traffic matrix, which tears down and reroutes unpinned connections but retains pinned connections whose routes you want to retain.

Procedure 6-2 Pinning or Unpinning an OCH or DCL Connection

  1. Open the Connection Browser (Network > Connection Browser).
  2. Under Traffic, select the layer of the connection you want to pin (DCL or OCH).
  3. The treeview lists all connections at that layer. The Pinned column indicates whether the connection is pinned or not.
  4. Right-click on a connection and choose Pin (for unpinned connections) or Unpin (for pinned connections).

    5. After you tear down a traffic matrix, all pinned connections of that matrix remain active.

End of Procedure 6-2
