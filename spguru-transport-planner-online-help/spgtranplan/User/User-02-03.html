The SP Guru Transport Planner Workflow

The SP Guru Transport Planner workflow is divided into four main phases:

  1. Creating Baseline Information—Physical topology, network properties, traffic matrices and cost parameters
  2. Designing Your Network—Run dimensioning, routing, and grooming actions
  3. Evaluating your Network—Analyze the amount of equipment installed, degree of node/line connectivity, wavelength usage, equipment and line costs, and so on
  4. Hardware Configurator (optional)—Configure the installed hardware in detail (based on user-specified data files) to define equipment down to the rack, card, and port level
