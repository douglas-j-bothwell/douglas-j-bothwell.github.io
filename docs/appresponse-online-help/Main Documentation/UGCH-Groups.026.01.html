Groups
The appliance automatically collects and organizes data, in the form of metrics, into two types of aggregates:
Preferred IPs are aggregations of metrics across various combinations of protocols, ports, servers, and URLs.
Groups (also called Business Groups) are aggregations of metrics across IP addresses or AS numbers, representing the aggregate data from all elements within the group.
There are two types of groups:
–
Built-in Groups that are created automatically by the appliance
–
Business Groups that are defined by the user with the exception of four preconfigured Business Groups
