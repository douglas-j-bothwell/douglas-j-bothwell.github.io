Director Group Table
The Director Group table displays metrics in numeric format for Director Groups. This includes implicit Director Groups (Business Groups and applications) and explicit Director Groups (total traffic and ISP) defined in the Director Group Manager. Director Group tables are only available on the Director.
To invoke a Director Group table, click the View menu, select Table and then select Director Group Table.

Use the Show drop-down list to select the desired Director Group type.
 
Table 3
Director Group Types
Type
Description
Business Group
Director Groups defined implicitly whenever a Business Group has Collect on Director enabled. If multiple appliances have Collect on Director enabled for the same Business Group, the traffic seen by each appliance is summed in the Director Group. However, it is still possible to drill-down from the Director Group to see the amount of traffic seen on each appliance.
The Applications drill-down from the Business Group only includes those Applications with Collect on Director enabled.
Total Traffic
Director Groups of type Total Traffic defined explicitly using the Director Group Manager. If the Director Group definition includes traffic from multiple appliances, the traffic from each appliance is summed in the Director Group. However, it is still possible to drill-down from the Director Group to see the amount of traffic seen on each appliance.
ISP
Director Groups of type ISP defined explicitly using the Director Group Manager. If the Director Group definition includes traffic from multiple appliances, the traffic from each appliance is summed in the Director Group. However, it is still possible to drill-down from the Director Group to see the amount of traffic seen on each appliance.

Business Group
application performance, network performance, utilization
All traffic between the Business Group and the Business Group (listed under connected groups) that also have Collect on Director enabled. The AppResponse Appliance automatically tracks all Business Group to Business Group communication.
The group you drilldown from is the “reference” group. The data for the groups connected to this group are summed over all the “Collect on Director” appliances of the “reference” group. For example, assume group A being collected from appliances 1, 2 and 3 while group B is collected from appliances 3 and 5. The traffic for the drill-down from group A to group B is calculated by summing the traffic of the A-B pairs from appliances 1, 2 and 3. Correspondingly, the traffic for the drill-down from group B to group A is calculated by summing the traffic of the B-A pairs from appliances 3 and 5.
Business Group >
IP Protocols >
IP Protocol
application performance, network performance, utilization
All Business Group traffic using a specific IP Protocol. Available IP Protocols are TCP, UDP, ICMP and Other IP Protocols. Other IP Protocols represents IP traffic that does not use TCP, UDP or ICMP at the transport layer. (e.g., IGRP, GRE). Drill-down on Other IP Protocols to see the list of non-TCP/UDP/ICMP protocols. Drill-downs to IPs is not available on the director. Further drill-downs to only defined applications are available from Business Groups > IP Protocols > [IP Protocol] > [Application].

application performance, network performance, utilization
All Business Group traffic using applications, including, defined, undefined and recognized applications.
