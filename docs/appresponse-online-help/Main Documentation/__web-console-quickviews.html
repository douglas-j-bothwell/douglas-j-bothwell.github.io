Quick Views
Quick Views provide high-level, “out-of-the-box” overviews of your data. With Quick Views, you can
Immediately discover top Business Groups and Applications, such as
–
Top talkers
–
Slowest apps
–
Most active apps
–
Business Groups with the most web-page requests
–
Business Groups with network issues (latency, packet loss)
Analyze specific Business Groups and Application to find key data such as
–
Top response times and delay components for an Application
–
Top clients and HTTP response codes for a Web Application
–
Top apps for a Business Group
Drill down into data of interest—many labels and chart elements act as links that load the relevant data into a separate Navigator tab
Figure 1
“Top Apps and Top Business Groups” Quick View
 
Note the following:
Quick Views are not available on Directors.
Some Quick Views are overviews that select their Business Groups and Applications automatically. Others are focused on a specific Business Group or Application that you select when you first open the Quick View.
In many Quick Views, the metric labels are abbreviated and do not show whether a metric refers to (Clients) or (Servers). Quick Views have the following general guidelines:
–
Metrics for Applications measure the Servers for those Applications.
–
Metrics for Business Groups measure the Clients for those groups
If you are not sure about whether a metric refers to (Clients) or (Servers), open the data in a separate Navigator tab and view the full metric names there.
To change the number of groups shown in a Quick View, hover the mouse over the right end of the card title and then click the Settings button that appears.
