Conference Quality Timeline
This Insight displays a graphical view of audio and video quality for all participants in a conference. All inbound and outbound channels of each call are shown over the duration of the conference. To open this Insight, right-click on a conference in the Conference Dashboard and choose View Timeline of Call Quality from the pop-up menu.
Figure 3 Conference Quality Timeline Insight
 
Note the following:
The graph colors reflect MOS scores for the call, observed at one-minute intervals. Quality thresholds are defined in the VoIP web UI > VoIP Data Settings > Call Quality Thresholds page.
The graph might show purple triangles. These highlight intervals when the appliance observed packet loss or packet discards significant enough that they might have an effect on call quality.
If the conference is currently active, you can use the Refresh button to update the data.
If a caller ends a call and then calls again, that caller will appear twice.
