ToS for VoIP Calls
You can use this insight to see the breakdown of calls by DSCP, to make sure that calls are assigned the proper DSCP value, and to view individual call records that are assigned to a specific DSCP. When you select a Business Group (left), a pie chart appears (right) with all call channels broken down by ToS (DSCP) value.
Figure 7 ToS for VoIP Calls
 
The following steps describe the general workflow for this insight:
1)
From the High Level VoIP Dashboard by Business Group insight, select a Business Group you want to investigate. Then, in the Troubleshooting Action pull-down menu, choose View QoS Breakdown of VoIP Traffic.
The ToS for VoIP Calls insight appears with a pie chart of all VoIP calls for the Business Group within the selected time window.
You can also open this insight directly from the Insights > VoIP menu and select a Business Group manually in the Business Groups table.
2)
Select a slice in the pie chart and click View Calls for ToS (DSCP). The High Level VoIP Dashboard by Business Group opens with the calls for the selected ToS category.
