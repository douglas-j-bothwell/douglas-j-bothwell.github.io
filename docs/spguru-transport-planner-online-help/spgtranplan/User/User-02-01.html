Overview

SP Guru Transport Planner enables network designers to create robust and cost-effective Wavelength Division Multiplexing optical networks. SP Guru Transport Planner's built-in network expertise provides capabilities for routing, grooming, and dimensioning networks to meet current and future traffic demands. You can create and test different "what-if" scenarios with varying topologies, traffic matrices, and configurations. Network managers can use SP Guru Transport Planner reporting features to compare the results of different scenarios, and thereby determine the most effective and least costly network designs to meet future demands. This chapter gives a brief introduction to many of these features, an overview of the design process, and references to additional information.
